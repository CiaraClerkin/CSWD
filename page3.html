Ai.Mi

Bio

Ai.Mi is a rogue A.I. who found herself in the real world, glitching her way to the top! She is great at medium range offense.

Primary Ability

For Ai.Mi's primary ability, she launches a circle projectile that grows bigger as it keeps moving. The projectile bursts (will hit the core or opponents if they are in range) if it goes as far as it can go, or, if you press your primary move button again, you can make it burst sooner. This move deals medium damage.

Secondary Ability

Her secondary move allows her to teleport and deal an heavy attack to enemies she comes into contact with.

Special Ability

Ai.Mi's special move allows her to place a turret which can knock back and damage enemies and hit the core in the straight line it goes in.

Asher

Bio

Asher is a protective older sister who is an unbreakable wall on the field! She is great at close-range offense.

Primary Ability

For Asher's primary ability, she deals a heavy hit to an enemy if they are in the centre of the attack. It deals a medium hit if enemys touch it.

Secondary Ability

For Asher's secondary ability, she dashes forward a bit with her shields up, and stops when she hits an enemy and deals a medium hit to nearby opponents.

Special Ability

For Asher's special, she combines both of her shields and launches them forward as a projectile. If they hit an enemy, they get bigger, move slower and can repeatedly deal medium hits to opponents. They deal less damage after the first hit and they can also be destroyed.

Atlas

Bio

Atlas is an orderly scientist who is experimenting with space time. He is great at defense and supporting his teammates.

Primary Ability

Atlas' primary ability launches a projectile that slows down as it goes and does a one medium hit to each enemy it touches.

Secondary Ability

Atlas' secondary ability makes a ring of light that grows and quickly goes away. The ring will continue to move and it will begin to shrink if it hits any enemies, doing a medium hit.

Era

Era is a shy mage who is best at supporting her teammates.

Era's primary ability is a beam that, when hits enemies, deals a light hit and they are debuffed, which reduces their size (the range at which they can hit the core with no abilities) and power (abilities have less of an impact).

Era's secondary ability gives her teammates a 40% speed buff, which runs out over 2.5 seconds.

Era's special ability creates a projectile that charges up to become bigger and stronger over time. You can release it before it fully charges, but it will deal light hits.

Juno

Juno is a friendly slime alien who uses her blobbles to redirect the core and damage enemies!

Juno's primary move shoots one of her blobbles. If it hits the core, it knocks it away faster than hitting it normally, and the blobble stays for a bit. If the core is hit into the blobble, it bursts and sends it moving.

Juno's secondary move makes her jump on one of her blobbles to travel and leaves it behind her. If the core is hit into the blobble, it bursts and sends it moving.

Juno's special move places all of her remaining blobbles down.

Luna

Luna is a chaotic child inventor who uses rockets she invented to blast away the competition! Luna is best at controlling the match from afar, so she is quite difficult to play, as you need to predict where your opponents and the core will be and time your moves right to get the most out of using her.

Luna's primary move launches a rocket. This rocket is more powerful the further it goes, so it's best to use it to hit enemies or the core from afar.

With Luna's secondary move, she rides one of her rockets until she hits someone or something. This move is also more powerful the further away it's used.

Luna's special is a missile strike from the air. She can place it up to a distance of about half of the map from where she is standing. The move has 2 zones within, the one in the middle deals high damage and the outer zone deals less damage. Opponents will not be hurt if they are not within the zones.
